Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and you'll receive a reset link within a few minutes.

Why can't I access the admin dashboard?

Admin dashboard access is restricted to users with administrator privileges. Contact your system administrator to request admin access.

How do I add new inventory items?

Navigate to the Inventory page and click "Add New Item". Fill in all required fields including name, quantity, price, category, and supplier.

Are notifications sent in real-time?

Yes, notifications for low stock, high stock, and system updates are sent in real-time. You can also configure notification preferences in your settings.
